Where We Sit
An experiment by Brian Foo at the NYPL's Broadway Hackathon

This simple interface takes data from the New York Philharmonic Subscribers Database from 1953 to 1980 and to show where one might sit in the concert hall based on where they live in New York City. Hover over the areas on the right, and the approximate seating areas will highlight on the left. The more seats that are positioned closer to the stage, the louder the music you will hear.
